Data Deletion Policy — Puzzix

Last updated: September 28, 2025 • Contact: support@puzzixapp.com

At MAHUGNON SERVICES LTD, we respect your data rights and apply a clear and transparent deletion policy.

1. Data Covered

  • Anonymous identifier generated by the app.
  • Level progression and gameplay statistics.
  • Push notification token.
  • Minimal technical history related to gameplay sessions.

2. Request Procedure

  1. Send an email to support@puzzixapp.com with the subject “Account Deletion”.
  2. You will receive a confirmation once the deletion is completed.

3. Deletion Timeframes

Your data will be deleted within a maximum of 30 days from receipt of your request.

4. Data Not Deleted Immediately

  • Security logs: retained for up to 90 days.
  • Legal obligations: longer retention if required by law.

5. Irreversibility

Deletion is final and irreversible. Once your data is deleted, it cannot be restored.

6. Contact

For any questions: support@puzzixapp.com
